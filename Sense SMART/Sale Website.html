The Best Adapted Smartphone

Sense SMART is a new start-up created in April 2021, which has developed a smartphone designed for people with deafness, mute or hard of hearing who have difficulty using their smartphone like any other person.
This smartphone is provided with an audio-visual assistant that helps to transform gestures into spoken or written word and vice-versa.
The smartphone also contains a range of applications that are specifically designed to improve the disability and daily life of these people.

A real solution for a real problem

The persistent problem that we want to create this smartphone for, is related to the fact that these people still don't have the possibility to talk comfortably in audio or video call, something that embarrasses them, especially when it is related to studying by phone as it is the case now with the Covid-19, these people really have a great difficulty to follow their courses normally, so if they want to do interviews on Skype, Zoom, Teams... or even learn things by videos they can't do it (we are talking about real cases that we have met, so it really exists, these people can't manage), so even for normal daily use, like watching videos without subtitles or talking to their family, friends and relatives as we all do, they can't do it.

For you!

Our main customers are the deaf, mute or even hearing impaired people who are not so critical but also need support:
1- Children from the age of 3, who are starting to know what a smartphone is since it becomes an indispensable necessity nowadays, and their brain still hasn't learned the standard method of other smartphones, so those childs will accustom their brains to the use of our smartphone first.
2- People who had to lose one or both senses accidentally and need to be accompanied to learn to adapt with their disability.
3- Or simply the elderly who have lost their senses with age.
